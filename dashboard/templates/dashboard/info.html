The Mission

Supreme Modern Physics was founded with a single goal: to make Physics the most enjoyable and easiest subject for students in Kenya and beyond. We believe that every concept, no matter how complex, can be understood when presented with clarity, visual aids, and a step-by-step logical approach.

Our resources are meticulously crafted to align with the 8.4.4 curriculum, focusing specifically on the high-weightage topics found in KCSE Paper 2.

The Revision Series

The "Form 1 – 4 Paper 2 Revision Series" is a comprehensive collection of workbooks designed to guide students from the fundamentals to advanced exam techniques.

Form 1 & 2 Base

Foundational concepts: Electrostatics, Magnetism, and Current Electricity.

Form 3 Mastery

Diving into Heating Effect, Light, and Geometric Optics.

Form 4 Modern Physics

X-rays, Radioactivity, Photoelectric Effect, and MRI technology.

Exam Revision

Past paper analysis and predicted exam-style practice questions.
